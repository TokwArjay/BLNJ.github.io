INTRODUCTION

"Jazon's Femboy Secretary" is a bot created for the Bahay-Learning ni Jazon (BLNJ) members to use.

It is developed by Paperplane, one of the members of the server and also its own admin. It was a side project started back at the summer of 2025, around July. It was meant to be a project that is meant to be a learning practice for Paperplane to further enhance his understanding of Python, Bot, Web Apps, and API applications.
